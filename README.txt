“The primary mission of Native Educational Endeavors (NEE) is to provide educational opportunities for American Indians, at any stage of life, and to create and support efforts that foster cross-cultural respect between native and non-native individuals, organizations and communities.” NEE Executive Director, John Henry Glover, JD.
